Privacy Policy for DialRolo

Last Updated: March 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy outlines the data handling practices of DialRolo. Our service is developed with a focus on user privacy, call log management, and user-initiated data portability.

Philosophy: DialRolo follows a "Local-First" approach. The application provides tools for users to manage, export, and import their own history according to their individual needs and preferences.

1. Data Access

To provide call log management and backup features, DialRolo requests access to the following information on your device:

  • Call Logs: Phone numbers, timestamps, durations, and call types.
  • Contact Information: Used to match phone numbers with Contact Names for display purposes within the app.
  • Device Information: Non-sensitive identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID) may be used for technical optimization and relevant advertising.

2. Data Usage

  • Display & Organization: Sorting call records based on local contact information.
  • Data Portability Support: Providing features for manual export and import of history.
  • Technical Performance: Utilizing non-sensitive data to maintain app stability.

3. Data Storage and Portability

DialRolo is designed to function without automatically uploading sensitive personal data to external servers. Data management is under the user's control:

  • Local Storage: All call logs are stored in a private database within the device's secure environment.
  • Export & Import Features: The app includes manual Export and Import functionality, allowing users to move their history between devices without relying on third-party cloud intermediaries.
  • Encryption Protocols: Exported files utilize industry-standard encryption to maintain data integrity during the manual transfer process initiated by the user.

4. Third-Party Interactions

Personal Data: DialRolo does not sell or trade personal call logs or contact names with third-party companies.

Advertising: Third-party advertising services (e.g., Google AdMob) may handle non-sensitive identifiers according to their own privacy standards to display relevant content.

5. Technical Security

DialRolo utilizes encryption for its Secure Export/Import features. These technical measures are designed to maintain the integrity of the user's archived files during the backup and restoration cycle performed by the user.

6. Data Retention

Users may delete their call history and any exported archive files at any time through the app settings. Once a user deletes data locally, it is removed from the device's active storage and cannot be recovered by the application provider.

7. Contact

For inquiries regarding this policy, please refer to the developer profile on the Google Play Store.
